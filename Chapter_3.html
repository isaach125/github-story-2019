Chapter Three: A Long and Warm Night

Following the smell, Lucas was sniffing and walking the same time and he found out that was actually the tantalizing aroma of flower scent! Lucas keeps approaching the giant tree and after a few minute walk, he surprisingly spots his target, the lost tribe! As a experienced journalist Lucas never forgets his purpose, he hold his breath and moved quietly and quickly like a panther, when he found a prefect place to take these valuable pictures, Lucas faint out!

Sun goes down, tribe people lit the bonfire and torches and start singing song to praise the season of spring, as they don't seems like care about Lucas. A few moments later, Lucas waked up, he frightening found himself in a tent, he tried to calm down and recalled what was he doing before he had passed out. At the same time, an old woman walks into the tent and explain to him. "You got fainted by the pollen. The nearby area is our sacred location for spring festival, the giant tree protects us from outsiders by releasing the pollen." She said. "The giant tree also shields our people and make us invisible, that's the reason why we are always disappeared. Actually, we work hard to keep the forest peaceful." The old woman said and invited Lucas to join the festival.

Lucas had spent a unforgettable night in his life. Eventually, he deleted all the photos and kept the precious memories to himself. -end-
