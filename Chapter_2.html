Chapter Two: Meeting The Old Tree

After long hours flight, the plane finally kissed the ground of the great Africa, Lucas' gaze took in the amazing landscape through the windows during landing and sighed deeply. "Spring is around the corner." he mumbled.

Lucas keeps sinking into the beauty of spring along the path to his accommodation, massive area of different kinds of trees had blossomed, composing a unique scenery with multiple colors. "Here we are, your destination Sierra Hotel." the driver said. Once Lucas has arrived his room, he quickly unpacks all his belonging, he goes out again with only his camera and gears and not wasting any seconds.

After a period of time of roaming in the city and asking the locals for some clues, Lucas has finally get the idea of where will the festival probably be held, now he aims at the ancient giant tree which is over five-thousands-years old, and the size of the tree trunk is big enough for people to walk through.

Lucas reach the entry of the woods, from now on he has to walk by himself to the giant tree. At the long walk, Lucas found the unpolluted nature is unbelievably beautiful, he loves the feeling of escaping the city, the spirit of spring fill he up with positive energy despite he still walking with his tired legs. At last, he meets the tree...
